Multi-Agent Aircraft Boarding Simulation

Plane boarding is inefficient and unpleasant even on the best of days. There are many different boarding methods that exist, each attempting to streamline the boarding process, but perfection is difficult to achieve.

We simulated these boarding methods using multi-agent environment within Unity in an attempt to find the best boarding method out there. Just like in real life scenarios, we incorporating crowd simulation ideas such as collision avoidance and queue formation while passengers stopped to stow their luggage and settle into their seats. The results may surprise you.

Written in C# with Unity.

EMG Spike Decomposition

Real-world EMG (electromyography) data can be messy and hard to interpret, but there's a wealth of information that can be discovered from it. Analyzing these firing patterns and characteristics of motor unit action potentials can provide important insights into a range of applications, from developing treatments for neuromuscular disorders to creating the future of human-computer interfaces.

This script takes in real, noisy EMG signal data and uses unsupervised machine learning and signal processing techniques to identify the different motor unit action potentials present in each recording and measure the firing patterns of each.

Written in Matlab.

Winner of the "Create a data visualization to navigate educational standards" on Mindsumo.
